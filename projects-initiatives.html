Projects & Initiatives

Mastering Data: Optimizing Collection and Analysis with Google Apps

The Late or Leave Early Log3 was developed utilizing Google Forms following discussions with colleagues and the administration team regarding the necessity for a streamlined method to monitor student attendance in the morning. A notable aspect of this endeavor is its automated data transfer to a dedicated Google Sheet, meticulously recording instances of tardiness and the precise arrival times of students. This data proves invaluable for parent outreach initiatives and serves as a focal point during staff meetings for comprehensive data analysis, enabling informed recommendations to be made.



Showcasing a Collection of How-to Guides for Various Digital Tools

  • How to Refresh Calendly Event - This guide provides a step-by-step walkthrough on utilizing Calendly for scheduling Parent-Teacher Conferences. I created this resource specifically to assist colleagues who are new to leveraging digital tools for organizing PTC appointments.
  • How to Setup Your Own YouTube Channel - This guide offers a detailed, step-by-step walkthrough for setting up a YouTube channel to host self-created teaching videos. It was developed as part of the resources for a collaborative professional development session that I co-facilitated with colleagues, focusing on using OBS for live streaming content.
  • How to Create Temporary Filter View - This guide is tailored for office staff following the introduction of the Late and Leave Early Log, aiding them in accessing the data collected via Google Form. Its purpose is to streamline workflow by enabling users to swiftly access specific data points.


Flipped Classroom Teaching Model

In 2014, I embarked on a journey to enhance math education accessibility for 6th-grade students and families through the creation of flipped classroom math videos. By flipping the traditional classroom model, I create video lessons for students to access at their own pace and convenience, empowering them to review concepts as needed. This approach not only allowed for more personalized learning experiences but also fostered greater collaboration and communication between students and their parents, who could now actively participate in their child's mathematical journey. Through these videos, I aimed to demystify mathematical concepts, promote deeper understanding, and ultimately enhance overall math proficiency among students.

All the math videos and curriculum resources are hosted in my class website Ms. Li's Math Page
